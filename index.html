    The Five Love Languages Explained

    Words of Affirmation

    Express love through verbal compliments, encouragement, and appreciation. If this is your love language, hearing "I love you" and receiving genuine praise fills your emotional tank.

    Quality Time

    Value undivided attention and meaningful togetherness. You feel most loved when your partner puts away distractions and focuses entirely on being present with you.

    Receiving Gifts

    Appreciate thoughtful presents as tangible symbols of love. The gift itself matters less than the thought, effort, and intentionality behind it.

    Acts of Service

    Feel loved when others do helpful things for you. Actions speak louder than words, and you appreciate when your partner eases your burden through thoughtful deeds.

    Physical Touch

    Experience love through physical connection and affection. Hugs, kisses, holding hands, and other forms of touch communicate care and closeness.
